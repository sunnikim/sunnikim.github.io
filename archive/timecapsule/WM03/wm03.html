EARTHLINGZ

This collective hopes to...

Increase the urgency to which people and governments respond to climate change.
Through a time-capsule that covers the last 20~ years of events. (late 1990s-2020).

THE RULEZ

1. Shock - shock with the truth

2. Green - green is not a colour, it’s a lifestyle

3. Urgency- Inspire urgency in others - Do it, do it right now!!!!

4. Anti-profit- Good climate is our profit

5. Anti-consumption - Re-use, Recycle, Revamp, repurpose.

6. Retrospective - Look forward into the past

7. Inclusive- Everyone is included

8. Accountability - Everyone is accountable

9. Collective - No Hierarchy

10. Appease - The climate with your actionz
