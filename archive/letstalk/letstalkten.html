Haven’t you been listening? Looks like you need more advice.
Being a good listener is a proactive job. You need to act as an
editor, encouraging stories to spill forth, finding interesting points,
summarising entire paragraphs and keeping the conversation on topic.

So, listening is an active role?

No, not always. Sometimes what the other person needs is
for you to just hear them out. Be comfortable with silence,
it can encourage them to open up. We shouldn’t always try
to relate or try to problem solve. We can let them just rant.

Of course, listening is only half of being a conversationalist.
Speaking is as difficult for some as listening is for others.
