Goodbye Digital Me



The moral of this narrative draws from Baudrillard’s understanding of the ‘Precession of Simulacra’. Where the signs and symbols of culture that construct our perception of reality has replaced it to become ‘Hyperreal’, signs as a reality in its own right. This is what we perhaps risk when we become too infatuated with our digital self, the signifier of our self. We will be lost in a twisted state of psychosis, the hyperreal, out of touch with reality but to a point where it does not matter.
