Smile At All Costs

protest posters / conceptual design / printmaking


Within our hyper visual culture, the codified symbols of happiness become more real than happiness itself. The flattened model of reality pushed forth by social media has subsumed all human activity such that even escaping the image has become a cliched trope.

As a result one have no choice but to surrender to hyperreality and slowly realise that most of reality is a human construction, one that is increasingly deluded and detached. Like caught in honey, disillusionment settles and the viscous trap of sweet happiness immobilises any revolt against the status quo.


Central Saint Martins BA GCD 2021
