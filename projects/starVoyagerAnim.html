This experimental game explores the duality of liberation and meaninglessness that stems from postmodern thought culture. The deconstruction of old models of authority, truth and power is an exhilarating prospect for those oppressed by cultural norms. Yet the feeling of anxious dread in the face of existential absurdity becomes palpable in collective psyche.

The garish exterior of the retro video game arcade is reminiscent of a time when the dreams of modernity materialised in the accomplishments of the space race. In humanity’s solemn realisation that life isn’t what was promised on the advertisements of modernism and capitalism, we find ourselves lost on a strange planet, one where we don’t belong. How can we find meaning again?

Collectively our faith in the hopeful dreams of modernity wavers.

As a postmodernity progresses, we live in disarray, disillusioned with the empty narrative of late-capitalism and disoriented by the lack of consensus reality.

Looking back, was the grandeur of space exploration, a symbol of humanity's dreams, just a spectacle simply motivated by ideology?
