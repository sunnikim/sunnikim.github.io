viist

a place where friends listen



This project was a social media app concept. I felt there was a lack of social media apps that were actually based around social interaction, instead they were mostly designed to maximised hours of attention spent on there to increase advertising revenue.

My service intentionally avoids these predatory design practices, instead opting to make design decisions that make for genuine social interaction and provide opportunities for the therapeutic and meaningful conversations that I believe can help improve the mental health of my users.
